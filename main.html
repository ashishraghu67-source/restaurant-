Concept

The Alchemist Table

Inspired by The Alchemist in Denmark, this menu treats the plate as a laboratory and the guest as a collaborator. Root, leaf, smoke, steam, metal, and light are all design tools.

Each dish is written as a chapter in a single story: ingredients entering the lab and leaving as memories. Every course carries its own narrative arc.

Storyline From Earth to Ether We begin in the soil with roots and leaves, move through grain and smoke, into sea and flame, and end with metal and memory. Plating is geometric and surreal, with floating elements and theatrical light to make each scene feel like a spell being cast.

Narrative Arc

One Story, Six Plates

The dishes are served in order so guests can feel the movement from earth to glass to sea to forged metal.

Service notes, pacing, and lighting shifts are designed to support the story, not just the flavors.

Chapter IV grows a miniature forest of plant-based forms. Chapter V opens the sea as a reflective surface. Chapter VI closes in metal, shadow, and heat‚Äîan experiment completed, leaving only memory on the palate.
